## Performance controls

- Identical era/resource/state assignments short-circuit instead of reallocating resources or queuing redraws.
- Vital cards do not rebuild StyleBoxes when their state is unchanged.
- The environment remains one code-drawn control with representative clusters and authored paths; no free cable physics, particles, or per-owned-unit nodes were added.
- Final worst-state host measurement: 294 nodes, 1,342.7 ms for 25 complete Build rebuilds, and 487.4 ms for 500 live refreshes.
