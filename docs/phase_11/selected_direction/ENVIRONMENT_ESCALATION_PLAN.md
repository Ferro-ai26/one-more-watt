# Soft Circuit Workshop — Environmental Escalation Plan

Status: Superseded by `../living_workshop/ENVIRONMENT_ESCALATION_PLAN.md`. Retained as historical continuity research only.

## Continuity rules

- Every era remains a layered 2.5D illustration of believable constructed infrastructure.
- WATT's cyan eyes and bolt-heart remain visible in the primary operating position.
- One earlier object survives each scale change as a physical callback, inset, labeled relic, or still-connected subsystem.
- New scale is revealed through a pullback, opened wall, window, roofline, map-table edge, orbital frame, or nested inspection view—not an unrelated style swap.
- Detail is produced at three density tiers: hero silhouette, phone-readable operating parts, and optional close-view wear/labels.
- Cosmic energy may glow only inside bounded conduits, glass, apertures, or active machinery. Space itself does not become a neon background.

## Eras 1–3 production evolution

### Era 1

- Monitor casing: desaturated green-gray painted/aged ABS, exposed rear vent, two mismatched feet, one service sticker.
- Face: two cyan squircle eyes and a small bolt-heart/status lamp; mouth line used only for pleased/completion.
- Purchases: wall outlet count, power strip, battery, fan, and cable visibly add or upgrade without literal hundreds of props.

### Era 2

- Retain complete Era 1 monitor silhouette; add bolted shelf, auxiliary status strip, external cooling fan, and cable crown.
- Face does not grow new features. Confidence appears through expression timing and steadier heart pulse.
- Environment shows safe/credible placement: generator outdoors, weatherproof entry, vented battery enclosure, roof equipment only where structurally supported.

### Era 3

- Bolt the original bezel into a 19-inch rack console; retain its proportions, wear chip, and eye spacing.
- Add rack status lights outside the face, never competing cyan eyes.
- Cooling, transformer, wiring, and backup equipment become architectural, while the old desk remains visible as a callback.

## Density limits

- At 320 logical pixels, show no more than five distinct environment silhouettes behind the focal content.
- Fine labels collapse to one colored band plus icon below 360 width; primary WATT/request copy never collapses.
- Wear/noise is omitted from 24–32 dp icons and reduced by 50% at the smallest layout.
- At least 12 dp clear space surrounds WATT eyes and every core numeric value.
