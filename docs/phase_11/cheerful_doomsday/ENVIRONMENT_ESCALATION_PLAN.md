# Cheerful Electrical Doomsday — Environment Escalation

Status: Approved Phase 11 direction reference under `DEC-028`. Era numbering and pacing are preserved exactly.

## Shared production grammar

- Five reusable scale kits: domestic (1–3), civic (4–6), territorial (7–10), planetary (11–13), cosmic (14–16).
- Four composited depth bands: shell/void, ordinary-world remnant, WATT infrastructure/cables, WATT/interactive foreground.
- Infrastructure states: `single → cluster → bank → facility`; counts remain numerical.
- Named anchors and authored curves replace free cable placement or per-unit rendering.
- Each capstone has `normal`, `overload`, `blackout`, `reboot`, and `expanded` capture states.

Across all eras, the operator authorization surface remains visually connected to the physical link being approved. Early payoffs emphasize repairs and reliability; middle payoffs emphasize blueprints, automation, infrastructure stability, and operator influence; late payoffs emphasize keeping dependent systems controlled; cosmic payoffs emphasize capability, prestige, and access to the next spectacle. These are narrative frames for existing rewards and unlocks.
