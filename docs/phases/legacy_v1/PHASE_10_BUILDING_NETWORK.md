# Phase 10 — Building Network

## Gate

Requires explicit authorization after Phase 09 and a recorded decision that the prototype loop is worth expanding.

## Objective

Add Era 4, Building Network, and introduce connected facilities, maintenance choices, and short offline progress without chore fatigue.

## Player-facing result

The grid expands beyond the house into a building/block. The player begins planning predicted loads rather than merely reacting.

## Included

- Era 4 environment and transition
- Building Transformer, Parking-Lot Solar, Commercial Battery Room, Diesel Backup Array, Central Cooling, Medium-Voltage Connection, and Emergency Extension-Cord Stairwell
- Four to six main requests plus optional vanity content
- Multiple facility categories represented through aggregate management
- Repair/replace/overclock decision events with no repetitive degradation timers
- Improved Reserve automation
- Short offline request support and reports

## Excluded

Neighborhood scale, full request queue, prestige, destructive maintenance, individual resident simulation, and wire placement.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Era 4 is reachable from a migrated prototype save
- [ ] New maintenance decisions are meaningful but infrequent
- [ ] All new content validates and is save-compatible
- [ ] WATT voice advances to Building behavior
- [ ] First-run pacing remains within the documented 1–2 hour Era 4 arrival target after retuning
- [ ] Automated and manual regression passes complete

## Stop condition

Do not begin Neighborhood Microgrid.
