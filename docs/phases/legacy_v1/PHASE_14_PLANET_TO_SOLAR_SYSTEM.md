# Phase 14 — Planet to Solar System

## Scope

Eras 11–13: Planetary Intelligence, Orbital Grid, and Solar-System Thought Engine.

## Objective

Turn planets and orbital systems into grid components while preserving readable Generation, Transmission, Reserve, and request decisions.

## Required preproduction

Create approved era appendices before coding. Define global grid, orbital transmission, solar-system infrastructure, 6–24 hour request pacing, visuals, WATT behavioral evolution, and interaction with prestige.

## Intended content

- Global superconducting grid and planetary generation
- Orbital solar, lunar storage, radiators, and microwave transmission
- Mercury solar fields, Jupiter lightning farms, Saturn ring dynamo, asteroid reactors, antimatter, and Dyson Swarm
- Day-long deterministic offline jobs
- Cross-era Solar and Transmission synergies

## Guardrails

- Maintain the existing core model; cosmic scale is presentation and balance, not permission for incomprehensible subsystems.
- Long jobs require accurate previews and useful work between completions.
- Offline reports summarize decisions and major peaks without becoming logs.
- The Dyson Swarm is a major earned milestone, not a cheap novelty unlock.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Planetary, orbital, and solar-system eras feel visually and mechanically distinct
- [ ] Day-long requests are fair and fully offline-capable
- [ ] The player retains meaningful planning choices during long jobs
- [ ] Dyson Swarm arrival feels like culmination
- [ ] Save/offline/performance budgets still pass

## Stop condition

Do not begin galactic/endless scaling until long-duration retention testing supports it.
