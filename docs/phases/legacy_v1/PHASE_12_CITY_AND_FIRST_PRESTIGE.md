# Phase 12 — City Data Center and First Prestige

## Objective

Deliver Era 6 as the deliberate transition to true idle management and implement optional WATT retraining.

## Player-facing result

WATT occupies a city-scale data-center campus. Major requests use 20–60 minute planning, authorization, offline execution, and performance reports. The player may retrain WATT to accelerate early eras.

## Included

- Era 6 infrastructure and environment
- Full long-request forecast/authorization flow
- Reliable request queue and automation controls
- Utility-scale generation, high-voltage Transmission, industrial Reserve, cooling district, and Optimistic Reactor Proposal
- First prestige preview, confirmation, reset transaction, Model Weight award, and initial permanent upgrades
- Fast-start behavior for repeated Eras 1–3
- Prestige-safe save backups and migration tests

## Excluded

Metro concurrent campuses, real-world reactor simulation, forced prestige, premium reset currency, and Eras 7+ content.

## Implementation requirements

- Continuing without prestige remains possible.
- Preview and actual Model Weight award are identical.
- Reset/preserved lists are explicit before confirmation.
- First prestige materially compresses the opening run.
- City request reports make offline preparation decisions legible.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Era 6 requests land in the intended 20–60 minute range
- [ ] Offline completion and queue behavior are reliable
- [ ] Prestige cannot partially apply
- [ ] Pre- and post-prestige backups recover successfully
- [ ] Permanent upgrade effects persist across runs
- [ ] Early-run compression is measurable and satisfying
- [ ] User reviews a go/no-go decision for long-game production

## Stop condition

Do not build Era 7 automatically.
