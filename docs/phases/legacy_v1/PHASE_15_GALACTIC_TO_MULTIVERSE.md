# Phase 15 — Galactic to Multiverse

## Scope

Eras 14–16: Galactic Utility, Universal Grid, and Multiversal Infrastructure.

## Objective

Deliver the absurd cosmic payoff and an extensible endless mode without invalidating the character-driven request loop.

## Required preproduction

Approve a late-game number strategy, prestige/endless economy, universe-modifier catalog, authoring plan, UI wireframes, and technical performance prototype before full content production.

## Intended content

- Multi-star Dyson infrastructure, pulsars, black holes, wormholes, and galactic Reserve
- Dark-energy turbines, cosmic-string Transmission, entropy recycling, and bottled Big Bangs
- Parallel universes with deterministic rule modifiers
- Alternate realities that reward Heat, reverse selected flow presentation, or treat comic infrastructure categories differently
- Endless scaling through controlled modifiers, repeated WATT versions, and universe goals

## Guardrails

- WATT remains the mission-giving character.
- Modifiers change decisions rather than merely multiplying all numbers.
- At least one viable strategy exists for each modifier combination.
- Large-number storage, formatting, saves, and calculations must be tested before migration.
- Infinite scaling must not require infinite authored content.
- Avoid random modifiers that invalidate offline plans without warning.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Cosmic eras retain readable bottleneck decisions
- [ ] Number representation survives projected endless ranges
- [ ] Universe modifiers are deterministic, previewable, and strategically distinct
- [ ] WATT dialogue still provides anticipation and callbacks
- [ ] Earlier infrastructure can re-enter relevance through late synergies
- [ ] Endless loops remain stable across save/load and prestige

## Stop condition

Do not declare the game release-ready; complete the release-preparation phase.
