# Phase 11 — Neighborhood Microgrid

## Objective

Add Era 5 and every automation tool required before the game becomes substantially slower.

## Player-facing result

The player runs several city blocks, forecasts 10–25 minute requests, and can safely leave the game while automation protects the grid.

## Included

- Era 5 environment and content
- Community Solar Farm, Municipal Generator, Neighborhood Substation, Battery Warehouse, Small Hydroelectric Plant, Underground Distribution, and Borrowed Utility Connection
- Load forecasting with useful confidence/detail
- Automatic repairs for non-strategic maintenance
- Reserve thresholds and safe throttling
- First request scheduling/queue rules
- Reliable offline simulation for the Era 5 duration range
- Four to six main requests and optional vanity content

## Excluded

City data-center campus, first prestige, concurrent regional jobs, randomized offline disasters, and monetization.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Player can configure the grid and safely leave during a request
- [ ] Automation explains its actions in the offline report
- [ ] Requests take 10–25 minutes when appropriately prepared
- [ ] Underbuilding slows rather than blocks progress
- [ ] All major pre-idle tools are unlocked before Era 6
- [ ] Migration, reachability, content, and device regression pass

## Stop condition

Do not begin City Data Center or prestige.
