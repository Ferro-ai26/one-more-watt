# Phase 20 — Planet to Solar System: Eras 11–13

## Objective

Turn Earth, orbit, and the solar system into grid components while preserving readable Generation, Transmission, Reserve, and request decisions across 6–24 hour jobs.

## Preproduction gate

Approve Planetary Intelligence, Orbital Grid, and Solar-System Thought Engine appendices before coding. Define global/orbital mechanics, infrastructure, request catalog, WATT evolution, environments, long-request behavior, prestige, and asset cost.

## Intended content

- Global superconducting grid, ocean thermal, mantle heat, lightning collection, and continental batteries
- Orbital solar, lunar storage, space radiators, and microwave transmission
- Mercury solar fields, Jupiter lightning farms, Saturn ring dynamo, asteroid reactors, antimatter, and Dyson Swarm
- Deterministic day-scale offline jobs
- Solar and Transmission category synergies

## Visual-production requirement

Cosmic scale must remain spatially understandable. Use scale transitions, clear grid paths, persistent WATT identity, and recognizable callbacks to early infrastructure. The Dyson Swarm receives a major authored visual payoff.

## Guardrails

- Preserve the core grid model unless an accepted decision proves insufficiency.
- Long requests show accurate previews and allow useful intermediate decisions.
- Offline reports summarize rather than dump event logs.
- The Dyson Swarm is earned, not a disposable joke purchase.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Three era appendices approved
- [ ] Each scale is visually and mechanically distinct
- [ ] Day-scale jobs are fair and offline-capable
- [ ] Player retains meaningful planning choices
- [ ] Dyson Swarm milestone feels climactic
- [ ] Production assets and licenses are complete for scope
- [ ] Save/offline/performance/device regression passes

## Stop condition

Do not begin galactic/endless production without checkpoint approval.
