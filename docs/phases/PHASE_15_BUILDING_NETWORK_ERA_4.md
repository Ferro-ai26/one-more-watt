# Phase 15 — Building Network: Era 4

Status: In Progress — Appendix Proposed; Implementation Awaiting Explicit Approval

## Objective

Expand the approved, production-skinned game into Building Network while proving that a new era can be created through data and established presentation systems.

## Player-facing result

The grid expands from one house into a building or block. The player begins planning around connected facilities, predicted load, and occasional meaningful maintenance choices.

## Included

- Approved Era 4 design/content appendix before coding
- Four to six main requests and optional vanity content
- Building Transformer, Parking-Lot Solar, Commercial Battery Room, Diesel Backup Array, Central Cooling, Medium-Voltage Connection, and Emergency Extension-Cord Stairwell
- Building-scale environment and WATT evolution
- Infrequent repair/replace/overclock decisions
- Improved Reserve automation and short offline play
- Complete Era 4 icon, effect, animation, audio, and asset inventory additions
- Migration from existing saves

## Excluded

- Neighborhood content
- Repetitive degradation timers
- Individual resident simulation
- Wire placement
- Permanent placeholder presentation

## Era art gate

Before implementation, approve Era 4 environment mood, WATT change, infrastructure silhouettes, visual motif, transition, new asset list, and reuse plan. All new screens use the established design system.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Era 4 appendix approved
- [ ] Era 4 is reachable from a migrated save
- [ ] New maintenance choices are meaningful and infrequent
- [ ] Requests fit the intended several-to-12-minute range after tuning
- [ ] WATT voice and appearance advance coherently
- [ ] No permanent gray-box asset remains
- [ ] Functional, visual, save, and Android regressions pass
- [ ] Manual playtest evidence recorded

## Stop condition

Do not begin Era 5.
