# Phase 23 — Final Production Art, Animation, and Audio

## Objective

Perform the final full-game consistency, completeness, optimization, and quality pass across all release-scope presentation.

## Principle

This is not the first skinning phase. Every era should already have production presentation. Phase 23 resolves cross-era drift, replaces remaining approved temporary assets, improves transitions and payoff moments, and optimizes the full package.

## Included

- Full asset inventory and placeholder audit
- WATT consistency across all forms
- UI component consistency across all screens
- Era environment hierarchy and transitions
- Infrastructure silhouette/readability review
- Major unlock/prestige/cosmic payoff animation
- Effects tuning and reduced-motion equivalents
- Complete audio mix, repetition variants, focus/pause behavior
- Haptic consistency
- Font/icon/license audit
- Texture/audio compression and APK-size optimization
- Full visual screenshot set

## Excluded

- New gameplay systems
- Large content expansion
- Changing approved direction casually
- Unlicensed or untraceable assets

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] No unintended placeholder asset remains
- [ ] All asset sources and rights are recorded
- [ ] UI and WATT remain coherent across all eras
- [ ] Major progression moments receive appropriate payoff
- [ ] Motion and effects respect accessibility settings
- [ ] Audio mix is non-fatiguing and functions with focus changes
- [ ] Performance, memory, and package-size budgets pass
- [ ] Full visual QA evidence is recorded

## Stop condition

Do not add new presentation scope except fixes found during release-candidate testing.
