# Phase 21 — Galactic to Multiverse: Eras 14–16

## Objective

Deliver Galactic Utility, Universal Grid, and Multiversal Infrastructure as the cosmic payoff and create a stable, extensible endless loop.

## Preproduction gate

Approve a late-game number strategy, endless/prestige economy, universe-modifier catalog, authoring plan, WATT cosmic arc, screen concepts, technical performance prototype, and asset plan.

## Intended content

- Multi-star Dyson infrastructure, pulsars, black holes, wormholes, and galactic Reserve
- Dark-energy turbines, cosmic-string Transmission, entropy recycling, and bottled Big Bangs
- Parallel universes with deterministic, previewable rule modifiers
- Alternate realities with strategically meaningful variations
- Endless scaling through modifiers, WATT versions, and universe goals

## Visual-production requirement

Cosmic scenes must avoid becoming indistinct particle noise. Each scale needs silhouette, hierarchy, restrained effects, readable grid flow, and WATT continuity. Universe modifiers have distinct but system-consistent visual treatment.

## Guardrails

- Modifiers change decisions rather than only multiplying numbers.
- At least one viable strategy exists for each approved modifier combination.
- Large-number storage, formatting, saves, and calculations are proven before migration.
- Infinite scaling does not require infinite authored content.
- Hidden random changes may not invalidate offline plans.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Late-game preproduction package approved
- [ ] Core bottlenecks remain readable
- [ ] Number strategy survives projected endless ranges
- [ ] Modifiers are deterministic, previewable, and distinct
- [ ] WATT continues to drive requests and callbacks
- [ ] Earlier infrastructure can regain relevance
- [ ] Production presentation is complete for all three eras
- [ ] Endless loops survive save/load, offline progress, and prestige

## Stop condition

Do not call the game release-ready. Begin the full-game content and balance pass only after review.
