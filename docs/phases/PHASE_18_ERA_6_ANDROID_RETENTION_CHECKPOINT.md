# Phase 18 — Era 6 Android and Retention Checkpoint

## Objective

Determine whether the complete active-to-idle arc, first prestige, presentation, and Android behavior justify production of Eras 7–16.

## Player-facing result

No major new content. The City build is tested, balanced, corrected, and evaluated as a coherent game milestone.

## Included

- Clean-save and prestige-run testing
- Android device matrix expansion where possible
- Request-duration and offline-return evaluation
- First-session and return-session observations
- WATT dialogue/repetition review
- Visual consistency across Eras 1–6
- Save upgrade/recovery and prestige transaction testing
- Performance, memory, battery, heat, and APK-size assessment
- Bug fixing within approved scope
- Explicit long-game go/revise/stop decision

## Evidence targets

- Time to each era
- Time and decision points leading to first prestige
- Early-run compression after prestige
- Offline return clarity and usefulness
- Most confusing and most compelling moments
- Player willingness to return for the next request
- Device-specific visual or lifecycle failures

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Eras 1–6 and prestige are reachable without debug intervention
- [ ] Active-to-idle transition feels intentional
- [ ] Long requests remain fair and understandable
- [ ] Prestige improves replay materially
- [ ] Production presentation remains consistent across all six eras
- [ ] No blocker/critical issue
- [ ] Known major issues have disposition
- [ ] Long-game production decision is explicitly recorded

## Stop condition

Do not begin Era 7 without explicit user authorization based on this checkpoint.
