# Phase 17 — City Data Center and First Prestige: Era 6

## Objective

Deliver Era 6 as the deliberate transition into true idle management and implement optional WATT retraining.

## Player-facing result

WATT occupies a city-scale data-center campus. Requests require planning, authorization, 20–60 minutes of online/offline execution, and performance review. The player may retrain WATT to compress earlier eras.

## Included

- Approved City content and visual appendix
- Utility-Scale Solar, Wind Farm, Hydroelectric Dam, Gas Turbine Plant, High-Voltage Transmission, Industrial Battery Field, Data-Center Cooling District, and Optimistic Reactor Proposal
- Four to eight City requests
- Full long-request forecast/authorization flow
- Reliable request queue and automation
- City environment, campus growth, WATT evolution, effects, and sound
- Prestige preview, exact reset/preserve disclosure, transactional reset, Model Weight award, and initial permanent upgrades
- Fast-start behavior for repeated early eras
- Save backups and migration coverage

## Excluded

- Forced prestige
- Era 7
- Premium reset currency
- Realistic reactor operations

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] City request forecasts are accurate enough for planning
- [ ] Prepared request durations fit 20–60 minutes
- [ ] Offline execution and queue behavior are reliable
- [ ] Prestige preview and award use the same calculation
- [ ] Prestige cannot partially apply
- [ ] Early-run compression is material and satisfying
- [ ] City production presentation is complete
- [ ] WATT's City behavior matches the character bible
- [ ] Save, device, and performance regression pass

## Stop condition

Do not begin long-game production. Proceed to the Era 6 checkpoint only.
