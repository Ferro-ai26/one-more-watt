# G01 Observation Worksheet

The informed project-owner session is a new-save baseline, not fresh-player evidence.

## 10-minute observation

- What does WATT want right now?
- What do you believe is limiting progress?
- Why did you make your most recent purchase?
- What felt like an actual choice?
- What was confusing, compelling, or tedious?

## 30-minute observation

- What does the current request category appear to test?
- How does Reserve differ from Stored Energy?
- What does changing allocation mode do, and why would you change it?
- Describe your current preparation strategy.
- Did any wait feel forced? If so, what action did you want to take?
- Was the latest report useful for the next decision?

## 60-minute observation or early stop

- What strategy did you use most often, and did you consider another?
- Which purchase or authorization felt most consequential?
- Which request categories felt mechanically different? Which felt alike?
- Were brownouts understandable, amusing, frustrating, or unnoticed?
- Did the visible world react enough to purchases and grid events?
- Is WATT appealing enough to motivate another request?
- Would you voluntarily authorize the next request now? Why or why not?
- What are the three strongest moments and three weakest moments?

## Evidence handling

- Paste the compact JSON summary.
- Attach or paste the human-readable summary.
- Keep interpretations in this worksheet; do not rewrite telemetry as emotion or intent.
- Record any coaching, interruption, crash, background interval, or incomplete observation truthfully.
